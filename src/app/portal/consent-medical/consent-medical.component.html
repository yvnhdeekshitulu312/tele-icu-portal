1. The nature of the Surgery/Invasive Procedures

  • Anesthetic Complications:will be discussed in the separate anesthesia consent form
  • General Complications: (May not apply to all surgery procedures) Bleeding - wound infection - chest infection -urine retention - leg blood clot (Deep Vein Thrombosis) - lung blood or fat clot - heart attack - stroke.
  • This operation's potential complications:

I understand all the above potential complications. I had the opportunity to ask questions and received answers to my satisfaction. I therefore consent for the above-mentioned Surgery/Invasive Procedure.
