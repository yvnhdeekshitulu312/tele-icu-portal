Post Conscious Sedation Instructions For Adult Patient / إرشادت عامة للمرضى البالغين ما بعد التهدئة الطبية الواعية

The medication which was given to me may be acting for the next 12-24 hours. It may cause drowsiness, tiredness, or confusion. It may cause clumsiness. Caution should be taken in daily activities for the next 24 hours.

قد يستمر سريان مفعول الدواء الذي تلقيته طيلة 12-24 ساعة القادمة كما قد يسبب لك الشعور بالنعاس والتعب أوالأرتباك. علاوة على ما قد تشعربه من تثاقل حركي. لذا فإن من الواجب مراعاة الحذر عند القيام بالأنشطة اليومية لحين انقضاء الأربعة والعشرون ساعة القادمة

Observation / Supervision Remain in the company of another responsible adult for the next 8 hours.
Activities Do not engage in any activity that requires alertness or coordination for the next 24 hours. This includes:driving, operation machinery , using power tools, cooking, climbing, riding a bicycle, swimming, hot tub, etc.
Making Decision Do not make any important decisions in the next 24 hours, such as singing contracts, making expensive purchases, or important commitments, etc.
Medications Take only medications prescribed by your physician, in the dose prescribed.Ask about resuming routine maedications such as insulin, blood pressure, or epilepsy medications, etc.
Seeking Help Seek medical help immediately at the nearest hospital for persistent vomiting, difficulty in breathing, strange or unusual behavior, confusion, or any other worrisome symptom
