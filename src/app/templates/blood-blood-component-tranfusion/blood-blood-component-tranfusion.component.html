بالنيابة عن اسم المريض

Authorise and give consent for the transfusion of blood or blood products.

The doctor has described the transfusion process and alternatives including consequences of refusing transfusion and explained the benefits that are expected from being transfused as well as the risk. I understand that although the blood products to be administered have been prepared and tested in accordance with strict scientific rules established by the MOH of KSA, following AABB standards, there is still a very small chance of allergic reaction & a very remote possibility of viral infection.

I had the opportunity to ask questions regarding transfusion of blood products for myself or for the patient and was given the right to accept or refuse transfusion.

With my signature I give consent to administering blood or blood products for myself or for the patient.
