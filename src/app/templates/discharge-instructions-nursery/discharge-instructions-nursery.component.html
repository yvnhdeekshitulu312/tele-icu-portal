Feeding Instructions

a) Breastfeeding– Breastfeeding is the best for your baby. Make sure to breastfeed your baby at least every 2 hours for 20-30 minutes on each breast every time you feed. Burped after each feeding.

b) Formula feeding average, a newborn drinks about 1.5-3 oz (45-90 ml) every 2-3 hours. This amount increases as your baby grows and is able to take more each feeding.

Observe the following:

a) Urine / Stool - should have at least 6-8 wet Diapers every 24 hours.

b) Always check your baby body temperature At least 2x a day. Normal body temperature should be 36.6-37.5 ◦ C.

c) Jaundice/Icteric or yellow skin & sclera

Visit your Pediatrician if you notice the following:

- Your baby skin becomes yellow, including the abdomen, arms and legs.

- The whites of your baby's eyes look yellow.

- Your baby becomes restless or sick or difficult to awaken.
